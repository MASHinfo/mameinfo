Regina plays Tempest at a theater at a time where she's supposed to work there, so her boss is a bit angry at her. She achieves a new Top-10 score, then realizes that some unknown DMK also has a high score registered. Next morning, she plays again and beats DMK's score. There are 2 notable goofs: First, when Regina beats DMK's score, she replaces his score with hers, instead of his score going one rank down; second, in the evening there's only a Tempest arcade game visible in the theater's lobby, while there are now three video games (Asteroids, Tempest and Asteroids Deluxe) the next morning...
